Resume

Hi, my name is Sasan Kayyod. I'm a junior designer and developer interested in Front-End web development. I'm currently based in Indianapolis and have two BFA's from Ball State University. Currently, I'm enrolled at 1150 Academy trying to beef up my coding experience to the level of my design skills. I have experience with a multitude of software ranging from the Adobe Suite to 3D modeling software and Unity 3D.

My other interests and hobbies involve video games and art. Which I send the rest of my time working. I'm part of the Mid-West Electronic Art Collective and have featured work in a number of areas in Indiana. My art involves making interactivity a key feature furthering my concepts to the viewer.
